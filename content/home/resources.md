Selected public resources from the lab:

- [XR Text Trove](https://xrtexttrove.github.io/) collects and organises techniques for text entry in extended reality.
- [Locomotion Vault](https://locomotionvault.github.io/) supports comparison and analysis of VR locomotion techniques.
- [ARME](https://arme-project.co.uk/) explores augmented-reality support for distributed music performance.
- [PrendoSim](https://prendosim.github.io/) provides a proxy-hand-based robot grasp generator.
- [Aging Touch](https://aging-touch.netlify.app/) shares work on tactile perception and ageing.
- [University of Birmingham VR Lab](https://virtualrealitylab.netlify.app/) showcases facilities and research activities.
